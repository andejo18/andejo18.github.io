Recital, February 1, 2013

A Philharmonic Fanfare - Eric Ewazen(b.1954)

A Philharmonic Fanfare was commissioned by members of the New York Philharmonic as a gift of thanks to Kenneth Campbell, a retiring board member. It was first performed in 1997 by trumpeter, Phillip Smith, hornist, William Kuyper, and trombonist, David Finlayson. As heard in this performance, Eric Ewazen's style of composition is easy to adapt for many different instruments.


Suite for Solo Tenor Trombone - Kit Powell(b.1937)

Kit (Christopher Bolland) Powell, a New Zealander, first composed Suite for Solo Tenor Trombone in 1961 and revised the work in 1991. With a condensed range, limited extended techniques, one of these unaccompanied movements would work well for strong players early in undergraduate studies.


Concerto Per Trombone e Orchestra - Nino Rota(1911-1979)

Nino Rota, famous as both a composer and conductor received the Oscar for Best Original Score for The Godfather II in 1974. A few years prior, in 1966, Rota wrote Concerto Per Trombone e Orchestra. Bruno Ferrari, a good friend of Rota, premiered the concerto. Ferrari helped the composer write this work specifically for trombone.


4 Impromptus for Low Bone Alone - Brad Edwards

In 2006, Brad Edwards, a composer, teacher, and trombonist himself, wrote 4 Impromptus for Low Bone Alone. Brad Edwards states, "I wanted to write pieces that would be satisfying to listen to and to play." Early in his career, Edwards taught at University of Northern Iowa(Cedar Falls, IA) and now teaches trombone at the University of South Carolina and performs second trombone with the Augusta Symphony.


Agnus Dei from Waisenhausmesse (k. 139) - Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart(1756-1791)

Agnus Dei (Waisenhausmesse, K. 139/47a) was written by twelve-year-old Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart in 1768 for the consecration of the Waisenhauskirche von St. Marx in Rennweg, Austria. The original orchestration contained a chorus(SATB), four vocal soloists, strings, two oboes, two clarino, two tromba, three trombones(alto, tenor, and bass), timpani, and basso continuo.

Lamb of God, who takes away the sins of the world,
Have mercy on us.

Lamb of God, who takes away the sins of the world,
Grant us peace.
